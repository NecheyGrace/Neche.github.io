Meet DR. okonjo IWEALA, Director Director-General WTO

Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala born on 13th June 1954, is a Nigerian economist and international development expert who has served since March1, 2021 as Director-General of the World Trade Organization. She is the first woman and first African to hold the office. She sits on the boards of Standard Chartered Bank, Twitter, Global Alliance for Vaccines and Immunization and the African Risk Capacity (ARC)
